About

Grant Harris is a London, UK based Designer who is driven by his passion for building meaningful products.

He currently leads product design at Beamery, disrupting the talent acquisition industry by building a next generation, innovative, recruitment CRM powered by machine learning.

Previously he worked at Intel as a Human Factors Engineer. There, he conceptualized and designed True Key, a digital identity manager that focuses on helping its users secure their digital worlds using biometrics - ultimately changing the worlds digital security standard.

He joined Intel as a part of the exiting team at PasswordBox, a startup which was founded in 2012. PasswordBox was acquired by Intel in 2014. At PasswordBox, he contributed as a Product Designer, helping users manage their online credentials.
